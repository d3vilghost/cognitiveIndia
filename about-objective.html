Our Prime Focus

We strongly believe in nurturing new talent and thus we provide opportunities to students and interns to work on industrial projects as a part of our team. With hands on skills and knowledge development we hope this young talent would contribute significantly to overall growth of industry and nation.

Cognitive Biotech & Food Research Private Limited is founded with a vision to provide technical and scientific expertise to food manufacturing, packaging, processing and biotechnology industry. Our main focus areas are training, education and research assistance to different sectors of industry.
